Third-party client for copymanga with better reading/downloading experience.
<h3>Features</h3>
1. Browsering the homepage, category, rank, my downloads, tag, and author.
2. Viewing. searching and reading the manga; remembering the reading progress of the captures of the manga.
3. Downloading the manga. But due to force majeure, the downloading is slow and error-prone. This is not due to bad optimization, absolutely not.
4. Reading the downloaded manga.
5. Checking update.
